Today, while sitting on a hill northeast of Lake Chabot and overlooking the San Francisco Bay Area, I got a call from Tesla to answer some questions pertaining to the job I applied for with that company. During that phone call I was able to schedule an on-site interview for Thursday at 1:30PM. After that, I walked to the United Postal Service store in Castro Valley to pick up the printout of my most recent Patelco bank statement. Tomorrow I will be using that bank statement printout in addition to my driver’s license as verification of my identity and residence in order to qualify for renting a small Post Office Box for my personal use at that same UPS store (and I have an appointment to do that at 11AM). For more information about what I have scheduled to do within the next two weeks, see journal entry # 337 in the Karbytes 2022 Journal section of this website.
